# Axis & Allies Game Tracker

## Project Overview

Axis & Allies Game Tracker is a digital tool designed to simplify and enhance the gameplay experience for Axis & Allies Global 1940 (Second Edition). The app helps players keep track of game rounds, IPC income, and bonuses, eliminating the need for physical markers and reducing the risk of errors during play.

## Background

Axis & Allies is a classic strategy board game series that simulates World War II on a global scale. Over the years, several editions and variants have been released, with Axis & Allies Global 1940 (Second Edition) being one of the most comprehensive and popular versions. The game is known for its depth, strategic complexity, and the need for careful resource management.

## Problem Statement

In the physical board game, players use paper money, chips, and markers to track IPC income, bonuses, and game rounds. This manual process can be fiddly, error-prone, and time-consuming, especially in long or competitive games. Mistakes in tracking can impact gameplay and player enjoyment.

## Solution

This project aims to provide a user-friendly digital app that:
- Tracks game rounds automatically
- Calculates and displays IPC income for each power
- Manages national objectives and bonuses
- Reduces manual bookkeeping and errors
- Enhances the overall Axis & Allies experience
